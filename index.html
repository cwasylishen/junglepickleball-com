About the Club

THE PREMIER CLUB IN THE SOUTHERN ZONE

Welcome to Jungle Pickleball, where passion for the game meets the Pura Vida lifestyle. We have expanded to offer 4 dedicated indoor courts, ensuring you can play comfortably regardless of the tropical weather. Located in the heart of Ojochal, we serve the entire Southern Zone community including Uvita and Dominical.
  • ✔ Weather-Proof Play: Our covered, indoor facility protects you from the sun and rain.
  • ✔ Stay Connected: Enjoy complimentary high-speed WiFi throughout the club—perfect for digital nomads.
  • ✔ Full Amenities: From our pro shop to our recovery zone, we have everything you need for peak performance.

OFFICIAL HONOLULU PADDLE DEALER

We are proud to be a top-rated dealer for Honolulu Pickleball Company. Don't play with worn-out gear—visit our pro shop to demo the latest technology, grab accessories, and upgrade your equipment right here at the club.

PREMIUM AMENITIES

The Jungle Kitchen

The heart of our social scene. Famous for our classic Root Beer Floats, fresh fruit smoothies, and energy bites. It's the perfect spot to cool down and connect after a match.

Ice Cold Recovery

Push your limits and recover faster. Members enjoy access to our 60°F Cold Plunge tank—the ultimate way to reduce inflammation and beat the jungle heat.

Pro Dink Wall

Waiting for a court? Sharpen your soft game on our dedicated practice wall. Perfect for drilling mechanics, warming up, or solo training sessions.
