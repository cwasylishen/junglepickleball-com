About the Club

THE PREMIER CLUB IN THE SOUTHERN ZONE

Welcome to Jungle Pickleball, where a love of the game meets the Pura Vida lifestyle. We have grown to four dedicated indoor courts, so you can play comfortably no matter the tropical weather. Based in the heart of Ojochal, we serve the whole Southern Zone, including Uvita and Dominical.

  • ✔ Weather-Proof Play: Our covered indoor facility keeps you out of the sun and rain.
  • ✔ Stay Connected: Free high-speed WiFi runs throughout the club, ideal for digital nomads.
  • ✔ Full Amenities: From the pro shop to the recovery zone, everything you need is on site.
